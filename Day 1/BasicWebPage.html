Beginner's Guide to Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence(AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn. AI is impacting industries from healthcare to finance

Types of AI

1.Narrow AI (weak AI)

This type of AI is designed for specific tasks like facial recognition, language translation, or spam filtering. Most of today's AI systems are narrow.

2.General AI (strong AI)

This type of AI is designed to mimic human intelligence and is used in fields like healthcare, finance, and robotics. Some of today's AI systems are general.

3. Super AI

This AI would surpass human intelligence in every aspect — from creativity to emotional intelligence (some believe it's not possible). Think of it like "Jarvis" from Iron Man!

Sample code

Here's an example of how a simple AI model is trained in Python:

    
        from sklearn.linear_model import LinearRegression

        # Training data
        X = [[1], [2], [3], [4]]
        y = [2, 4, 6, 8]

        # Model
        model = LinearRegression()
        model.fit(X, y)

        # Prediction
        print(model.predict([[5]]))  # Output: [10.]

Note : the <code> tags help preserve formatting for developers
